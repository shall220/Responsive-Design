What is Responsive Design?

Responsive Design is a way of designing web pages so they can be viewed on different devices and environments while trying to maintain a nice presentable look and feel.

Next

Why should you be concerned about Responsive Design?

  • It helps reach more users. “Day by day, the number of devices, platforms,and browsers that need to work with your site grows. Responsive web design represents a fundamental shift in how we’ll build websites for the decade to come.” - Jeffrey Veen Polacek, n.d
  • Less labour in creation."An efficient and effective way of adapting to the mobile screen is through Responsive Web Design." Leland, 2012
  • Websites are cheaper and easier to maintain.
  • It is a big step in accessibilty.

My list of Responsive Design tips

  • Keep it simple and easy to control.
  • Strict formatting helps with maintenance.
  • Responsive Design is almost always a good idea!
  • Older browsers may not work well with responsive design.
  • Simple lean web pages are easy to maitain as a web designer and easy to follow as a consumer.
  • With all the benefits learning responsive design is well worth it.
